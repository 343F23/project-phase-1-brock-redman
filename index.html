Welcome to Finance Tracking!

This web application allows you and businesses to keep track of all their spending, money currently at
your disposable in savings and track potential charity opportunities through the viewing of text and
graphs. Keep reading for a brief description of what you can expect to be able to do with this application.

To get started, you'll notice several tabs, home which will take you back here, and a
Finance tabs containing spending, saving and charity options. It's here that you will be able to track
these types of opportunities, as well as create, update, view, and delete opportunities. The visual
tab will allow you to add visual represtations of the current data you choose. Site inspirations from Northwest Federal Credit Union
