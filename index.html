Welcome to Finance Tracking!

This web application allows you and businesses to keep track of all their spending, money currently at
your disposable in savings and track potential charity opportunities, as well as create visuals to track
investment opportunities. Keep reading for a brief description of what you can expect to be able to do
with this application.

To get started, you'll notice several tabs, a Finance dropdown containing spending, saving and
charity options. It's here that you will be able to track these types of opportunities, as well
as create, update, view, and delete opportunities. The visual tab will allow you to track investment
opportunities by creating graphs that show a list of the daily highs of your chosen stock.
Site inspirations from Northwest Federal Credit Union
